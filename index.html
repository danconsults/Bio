I am passionate about informatioin logistics. To be specific, It's quite interesting to observe the flow and storage of information that is stored on databases. Blog section

  • Technology and Governments

    Are current centralized technologies mature enough to handle governments as users?.

    The Big challenge with governments during the austerity period is to stay lean while gathering more resources to sustain the economy. These challenging times are also opportunities because governments are pushed into innovatons like new technologies. This is especially since ruling parties and opposition are more prone to listen to each others' ideas.

    The concept of government and technology is a huge area, but when we look at the low-level government that controls access to citizen services such as health and security in an area, innivations like a private community-focused cloud can really add value.

    The risk of sensitive data being breached is still a big issue, but it has receieved too much attention than it deserves from media. There will always be challenges to moving to a new area, but with the right kind of training and proper acess-restrictions, governments can run more efficiently and also at a cheaper cost. That may not make a huge difference if you don't see the engines behind the services, but it should make the mayor happier to konw that their budgets are getting more managable.

Using R-language to Parse large amounts of text
Daniel M,
R-programming As assisted from the Book about R-programming in everyday use

Abstract

Substantial amount of research has examined the best methods of dealing with the massive amounts of data created everyday. Growth in better data storage technology and cheaper centralised systems have not changed the way companies capitalize on the information.

The main issue is that there's a big divide between the IT and Business - end aspects of comapnies dealing in big-data. My efforts here are to use R-programming language to analyse a large amount of text-data, and eventually gauge the difficulty-level of using the statistical language to analyse data. The aim is to uncover relationships within the text that would otherwise have taken a long time to uncover through reading.

Sample applications of this method can be used by product managers to summarize the actual feedback a product receives in the review section of an e-commerce website. Aside from the 1-5 star-rating methods, the real rating is in the words that customers use to describe the service-value your product offers.
